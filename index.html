This Website

This website provides a beginner friendly guide on the basics of Neural Networks. It tells you What Neural Networks are, how they function, Neural Networks in practice, and some further reading on Neural Networks for people who are interested. This website aims to get you up to speed with Neural Networks in the current AI landscape. Please refer to the home bar for more information.

Importance

As the world moves towards artificial intelligence, it has become crucial to have a basic understanding of neural networks. Neural networks and AI in general can make generalizations, analyze complex models of data, and rapidly classify information. They are the foundation for prevalent language models such as ChatGPT (shown later), Google Bard, and much more.
